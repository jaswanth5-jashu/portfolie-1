MediAssist Health & Management System

About the Project

The MediAssist Health & Management System is designed to simplify patient care by managing patient records and intelligently suggesting medicines based on symptoms and disease information. The system analyzes patient data, identifies the condition, and recommends suitable medicines using a structured medical database, helping users receive accurate guidance quickly.

In addition to medicine suggestions, MediAssist also provides nearby medical shop details, including address, contact information, and real-time medicine availability, making it easier for patients to find required medicines without searching manually. The system maintains complete patient histories, diagnoses, prescriptions, and visit logs to support better treatment planning.
